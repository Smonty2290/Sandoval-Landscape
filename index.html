About Us!

Sandoval Landscape & Build is a full-service outdoor construction company dedicated to transforming properties into functional and visually appealing spaces. We specialize in landscaping, hardscaping, landscape renovation, design, and ongoing maintenance. With a strong focus on quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and dependable service, we remodel outdated landscapes and build custom outdoor features that add long-term value and curb appeal. Our goal is to deliver clean, durable, and well-designed results tailored to each client’s vision.

Services

Landscape/Hardscape

Custom outdoor solutions to enhance curb appeal and functionality. From grading and sod to planting and drainage, we create clean, durable landscapes built to last.

High-quality patios, walkways, retaining walls, and outdoor features. Built with precision and durable materials to add long-term value to your property.

Renovation

Complete landscape remodeling for outdated or damaged outdoor spaces. We remove, redesign, and rebuild old landscapes into modern, functional designs.

Design

Professional landscape and hardscape designs tailored to your vision and budget. Smart layouts that maximize beauty, function, and long-term value.

Maintenance

Ongoing landscape care focused on detail and cleanliness. Trimming, edging, cleanups, mulching, and seasonal services to keep your property looking its best.
